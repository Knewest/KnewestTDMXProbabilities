Knewest TDMX Probabilities

Made to better understand the RNG probability of the game.

Equations used (click)
Percent to probability
p=p%100
Chance in N opens (N)
P(≥1)=1−(1−p)N
Milestone opens for chance g
N(g)=⌈ln(1−g)ln(1−p)⌉
Base to Godly Hunter difference
Δ = p GH − p Base m = p GH p Base .

The toggle changes p (the probability used in every formula).
Luck potions multiply p on top of Base/Godly Hunter odds.
